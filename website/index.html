Her Story

Anne Frank was a German-born Jewish girl who kept a diary in which she documented life in hiding under Nazi persecution during World War II. She is a celebrated diarist who described ordinary thoughts and feelings in extraordinary times.

For two years, she and her family hid in the "Secret Annex" in Amsterdam. Her diary, which she received for her 13th birthday, chronicles her development from a girl into a young woman, offering a poignant and personal perspective on the Holocaust.
